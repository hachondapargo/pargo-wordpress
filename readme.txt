The most convenient logistics solution to offer to your customers to pick up their parcels when it suits them best.

== Description ==

Pargo is a logistics solution that lets consumers collect and return parcels at dedicated pick-up points throughout the country. Businesses use Pargo as an additional logistics service next to home deliveries which they offer through couriers. Pargo allows you to add click-and-collect as a delivery option and improves your value proposition to your customer. Integration for all woocommerce platforms, no special programming required!

= Features =

* Lower cost per parcel
* Unique returns solution without the trouble of home collections
* Better service provision through secure and transparent IT tracking
* Improved support with extended operating hours and no strikes
* Viral consumer marketing through word-of-mouth recommendations
* Over 1500 pick-up points across every town in South Africa
* Most stores open 24/7
* Urban, regional and rural presence
* Well-known and trusted South African stores: Freshstop at Caltex, Waltons, Shell, OK Grocer, 7Eleven, Mr.Video, Vee's Video,
Spar and local individual owned stores
* Partners have a combined amount of over 2000 stores where we are establishing pick-up points in the coming year
* Easy installs directly into the left WordPress Menu

== Installation ==

= Requirements =

* PHP 5+ (same as WordPress 4.0.1)
* Wordpress: 4.0.1+

1. Upload the 'pargo-shipping' folder to the '/wp-content/plugins' directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
